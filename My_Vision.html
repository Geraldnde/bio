My Journey of Dreams

As a child, I dreamed of going to the moon. I had no money, no wings, only imagination. But as I grew, that dream became clearer: the moon wasn’t just for me—it was for everyone I could reach. Every fall and every rise taught me that the true dream is not to reach the moon, but to give the moon to others.

Today, through art, I use colors, pencils, and imagination to help little ones express themselves in ways they will never be judged. It is a language that speaks from the heart: “I see you, and I know what you’ve been through.” This is my vision—to create a space where voices, feelings, and creativity are never silenced.

I am excited to share this journey with you. Let’s explore together.
